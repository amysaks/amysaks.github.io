Synopsis

The main character, Cady Heron, just moved to a public highschool from being homeschooled by her parents in Africa. When she arrives, she learns that highschool is far from what she imagined. Growing up as an only child, Cady didn't get to develop most social skills the other kids have. Not knowing what is best, she lets popularity get the best of her when she finds herself in the most exclusive group at the school called "The Plastics."
