"My Heart and Other Black Holes" by Jasmine Warga

 This book is about a 15 year old girl named Aysel. Aysel believes that her life is horrible and falling into a black hole. Aysel finds this boy Roman, on a suicide website, where she becomes friends with him. They both have the same intentions, but they need eachother to make sure they don't decide otherwise. Aysel and Roman develop a strange friendship which helps navigate the book. You will have to read "My Heart and Other Black Holes" to find out how it ends!

Main Character Descriptions

Aysel

Aysel is a 15 year old girl with depression. She has an obsession with planning her own death. Aysel fears of growing up like her deliquent father, and thinks its the best for everyone if she just disappeared. Ayesl is shy, reserved, and Langston, Kentucky.

Roman

Roman is Aysel's suicide partner. She finds him on a suicide website and decides to contact him. Roman has a specific date of death in mind. He connects with Aysel and they start filling eachother "black holes". He lives nearby Aysel's home town.

Why is this my favorite book?

"My Heart and Other Black Holes" by Jasmine Wang is my favorite book because it is easy to understand and keeps you wanting to read more. The book is not only written beautifully, but portrays a strange connection between two outcasts who share a bizzare bond. I reccomend this book to many teens because it shows how a connection with someone could possibly save your life. I have read this book a few times, and I could read it a few more too.
